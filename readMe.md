Avoid small type, as individuals with dementia often struggle to track and read long, dense walls of text.Orienting Headers: Since people with dementia can quickly lose track of time or location, ensure the app always displays a simple, prominent, and permanent clock and location indicator on the home screen.3. Safety and FamiliarityPhoto-Based: Because short-term memory fades but long-term emotional recognition remains, emphasize visual posts. For instance, replace text-based thread titles with photo-based buttons or avatars of familiar friends and family members.Curated Content: Limit access to outside forums. The platform should be locked down to "walled gardens" of pre-approved, uplifting content, such as personalized music therapy playlists or pre-saved memory books.Voice-Based Chatbots: Integrate conversational AI features that can read posts aloud or gently redirect the user if they are confused.
